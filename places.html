Top Attractions In Lucknow

Bara Imambara :

The first one in the list is the Bara Imambara. It is a shrine complex built by Nawab Asaf-ud-Daula in 1784. Known for its Maze.

Chota Imambara :

A stunning monument built in 1838, also called the 'Palace of Lights' due to its profuse decorations during events.

Hazratganj :

Do you love shopping? Hazratganj Market is the answer. It is the central shopping complex of Lucknow with a variety of shops.

Rumi Darwaza :

The Rumi Darwaza is an iconic specimen of Awadhi Architecture. Built in 1784, it has a 60 feet high entrance.

Janeshwar Mishra Park :

This lovely park, named after the late Janeshwar Mishra, a well-known political figure, is a retreat for nature enthusiasts and a popular destination for locals and visitors.

Ambedkar Memorial Park :

The park was constructed to commemorate those who dedicated their life to equality and human justice.
